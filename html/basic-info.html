His Early Life

Xu Xin was born on January 8, 1990, in Xuzhou, Jiangsu Province, China. He began playing table tennis when he was 5 years old and got his first championships in 2004. He then enrolled in the National Second Team in February 2006 and promoted to National First Team in the same October.

Playing Style

Young Xuxin

Xuxin is famous for his untraditional penhold grip and his powerful and spinny forehand loops, His footworks and his creativities in tactics can often surprise everyone, when you are expecting him to use his backhand, he can perform a decent behind-the-back shot and ultimately earns the point. The title of "People's Artist" was just earned like that. He has also demonstrated an exceptional control on mid-to-long distance shots, he frequently pulls off high lobs from afar to stay in the rally, turning defense into attack and then winning the point.

Personality & Off the Table

Outside of the table, Xu Xin is known for his humour. During many after-match interviews and separate interviews, his responses can show his professionality with no damage in his humours.
